## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Discover amazing events along your railway journey!**

StationScout helps you find Ticketmaster events happening near UK train stations. Whether you're commuting, traveling for leisure, or planning a day out, discover concerts, sports matches, theatre shows, and more - all within easy reach of the train network.

**How It Works**

1. Choose your Train Operating Company (e.g., GWR, LNER, Avanti)
2. Select a railway line
3. Browse stations along the route
4. Discover events within 50km of each station
5. Book tickets directly through Ticketmaster

**Key Features**

- Browse events by train line and station
- View event details including dates, venues, and images
- Book tickets directly via Ticketmaster
- Save favourite events to your bookmarks
- Share events with friends and family
- Works offline - browse saved events without internet
- Explore events by UK region (London, Manchester, Edinburgh & more)

**Supported Train Operators**

All major UK train operating companies including:
- Great Western Railway (GWR)
- LNER
- Avanti West Coast
- CrossCountry
- Northern
- TransPennine Express
- Southeastern
- Southern
- And many more!

**Event Types**

Find tickets for:
- Music concerts and festivals
- Premier League and Championship football
- Rugby matches
- Theatre and West End shows
- Comedy nights
- Family entertainment
- And much more from Ticketmaster

**Plan Your Journey**

Combine your train travel with entertainment. StationScout makes it easy to find what's on near your destination, helping you make the most of every trip.

**Privacy First**

- Your data stays secure with encrypted authentication
- Bookmarks are stored locally on your device
- We never sell or share your personal information

Download StationScout today and start discovering events along your railway journey!
