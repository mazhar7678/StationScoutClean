# Privacy Policy for StationScout

**Last Updated: December 25, 2025**

## Overview

StationScout ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application that helps users discover Ticketmaster events near UK train stations. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address**: Used for account creation and authentication
- **Password**: Securely stored and encrypted via Supabase authentication

### Usage Data
- **Bookmarked events**: Stored locally on your device for offline access
- **App preferences**: Your selected train operators and lines

### Location Data
- We do **not** collect or store your precise location
- Station locations are pre-loaded in the app to calculate event distances

## How We Use Your Information

- **Authentication**: To create and manage your account
- **Personalization**: To save your bookmarked events
- **App functionality**: To show events near your selected stations

## Data Storage

- **Account data**: Stored securely on Supabase servers (EU-compliant)
- **Bookmarks**: Stored locally on your device only
- **Event data**: Sourced from Ticketmaster API, cached locally for offline use

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Supabase | Authentication & database | [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy) |
| Ticketmaster | Event data | [ticketmaster.co.uk/privacy](https://www.ticketmaster.co.uk/privacy) |
| Expo | App infrastructure | [expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy) |

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- When required by law
- To protect our rights or safety

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained until you delete your account
- Bookmarks are stored locally and removed when you uninstall the app

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your bookmarks

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Children's Privacy

StationScout is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure password hashing
- Protected database access

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
